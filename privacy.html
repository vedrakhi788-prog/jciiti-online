Privacy Protection & Data Security

Last updated: 2026-01-06

Overview

Jack Travels values the confidentiality of every traveler and travel partner. We restrict data collection and maintain only essential details for booking administration and services.

Categories of Information Collected

  • Competition details: Sign-up materials and participant specifics needed to manage competitions and titles.
  • Direct contacts: Information provided through inquiry forms to help us respond to participant messages or organizational requests.

Browser Tracking

We maintain minimal tracking tools for website performance and user analytics. We maintain strict security for every participant's identity.

Control & Preferences

  • Reach out to request review or withdrawal of personal information associated with competition registration or inquiries.
  • Unsubscribe from messages by adhering to unsubscribe options in every correspondence.

Reach Out

For data security inquiries or privacy-related questions, please use the contact form on the main page or reach our support desk.
